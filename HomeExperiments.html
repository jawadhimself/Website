    Warning:

    Safety must always be the top priority before starting any science experiment. Before gathering materials, ensure an adult is present to supervise, especially for projects involving heat, sharp objects, or household cleaning agents. Always protect your eyes by wearing safety goggles, and keep long hair tied back. Work in a clean, designated space away from food, and immediately clean up any spills. Lastly, know the proper disposal method for all materials once the experiment is complete.

    - The Dancing Raisins Experiment

    This experiment is about how a raisin can dance up and down in a glass of sparkling water or soda.

    Materials Needed:

    • A clear glass or jar
    • Raisins
    • A carbonated drink like sparkling water or soda

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    First Step:

    - Fill the glass with your carbonated drink

    Second Step:

    - Drop a few raisins into the glass, and let them sink to the bottom

    Third Step:

    Let them sink to the bottom

    Result:

    Congratulations, you have a Dancing Raisins that goes up and down!
