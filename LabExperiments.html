    Warning:

    Safety must always be the top priority before starting any science experiment. Before gathering materials, ensure an adult is present to supervise, especially for projects involving heat, sharp objects, or household cleaning agents. Always protect your eyes by wearing safety goggles, and keep long hair tied back. Work in a clean, designated space away from food, and immediately clean up any spills. Lastly, know the proper disposal method for all materials once the experiment is complete.

    - The Lava Lamp Experiment

    This experiment is about creating a bubbly, rising, and falling "lava" effect in a clear container.

    Materials Needed:

    • A clean, clear plastic bottle or tall jar
    • Vegetable Oil
    • Water
    • Food Coloring
    • Effervescent Tablet (like Alka-Seltzer, broken into small pieces)

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    First Step:

    - Spill the oil inside the empty bottle

    Second Step:

    - Spill water with the same amount of oil has been spilled, and put some drops of the food colors

    Third Step:

    Now, we put the Effervescent tablet in the bottle

    Result:

    Congratulations, you have a Lava Lamp
